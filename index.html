The Housepital

With the Housepital, you will no longer need to travel to access care, the care will come directly to your home !

Discover

The Housepital was founded two years ago by a young student who quickly understood the difficulty for the French population living in rural areas, to go to hospitals in order to have access to care. The lack of space combined with the fact that the hospitals are often several tens of kilometres away from their homes makes the task very difficult. The Housepital is here to solve this major problem of the French society by offering home care financed by hospitals. Based in three French cities, we are here to solve this major problem.
